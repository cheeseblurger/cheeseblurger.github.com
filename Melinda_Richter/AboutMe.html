About Melinda

 

Melinda Richter grew up in Hamilton, Ontario. A lover of art, music, culture and heritage, Melinda is an active member of her community’s arts and culture scenes. With a BA in Religion and Culture from Wilfrid Laurier University and an MA in Religion, Ethnic, Immigration and Pluralism studies from University of Toronto, Melinda topped off her education with a post-graduate certificate in Museum Management and Curatorship from Sir Sandford Fleming College in Peterborough, Ontario and Personal Property Appraisal Accreditation from the Canadian Personal Property Appraisers Group. She has led or assisted in various projects combining arts and cultural history, including a retrospective photography exhibit for the Ontario Jewish Archives in Toronto and a community arts exhibit about the working history of the Jewish community in Hamilton. For the past four years, Melinda has worked with The Arctic Experience McNaught Gallery as a consultant for fine art and antique appraisals, collection management, framing, and conservation. Attracted by its vibrant arts scene, rich historical heritage, and proximity to nature, Melinda recently returned to Peterborough. Through a unique partnership with The Arctic Experience McNaught Gallery, Melinda is proud to offer Peterborough a full range of the gallery’s services.
